Partnership Tiers
Tier I

Principal

Founding members of LVO Holdings; equity holders in the parent entity with full governance rights and participation in profits. Restricted to family members only.

Tier II

Associate

Trusted professionals in the LVO orbit. Attorneys, CPAs, contractors, lenders, and more. Preferred access with consistent business and no equity.

Tier III

Partner

Co-investors brought in deal by deal through LVO Alliance. Earn deal-level equity. Advancement is based on performance, not position.

Tier IV

Member

Starting position within the LVO community. Prove your worth and climb the ranks.
